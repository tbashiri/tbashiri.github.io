I am a PhD candidate in Physical Chemistry at the California Institute of Technology working in laser spectroscopy and molecular spectroscopy.

My research focuses on high-resolution spectroscopy of calcium-containing species, including calcium isotope measurements and spectroscopy of CaF isotopologues. I am broadly interested in AMO physics, chemical physics, precision measurement, and experimental spectroscopy.

Current projects include:
- Calcium isotope spectroscopy
- CaF electronic spectroscopy
- Spatial heterodyne spectroscopy
- High-resolution experimental methods

Please see the Research and Publications pages for more details.
